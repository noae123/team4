Winnie the Pooh once said:
you dont spell love, you feel it.
And what better way to feel if not through music?
Here you will create a greeting card, in which you will upload an audio file of any music you choose.
In addition, in the greeting card you can select the image you want to add and choose from a variety of animations that will move along with the music.
